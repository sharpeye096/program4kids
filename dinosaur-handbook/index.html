Key Concepts

Here are some important words you should know to become a dinosaur expert:

🌿 Herbivore

Dinosaurs that only ate plants. They usually had flat teeth to chew leaves! (Like the Triceratops)

🥩 Carnivore

Dinosaurs that only ate meat. They had sharp teeth and strong legs to hunt. (Like the T-Rex!)

🦴 Fossil

The remains or impression of a prehistoric plant or animal embedded in rock and preserved in petrified form.

☄️ Extinction

When an entire species of animal dies out forever. Dinosaurs went extinct 65 million years ago!

Story Time & Facts

Did You Know?

The Stegosaurus had a brain the size of a walnut! But it had heavy armor and spikes on its tail to defend itself.

The Great Meteor

Around 65 million years ago, a massive asteroid the size of a city crashed into Earth! This event changed the climate so much that the dinosaurs could not survive. But wait... some smaller feathered dinosaurs survived and evolved into the birds we see outside our windows today!
